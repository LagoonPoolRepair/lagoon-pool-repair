About Us

At Lagoon Pool Repair, we specialize in equipment repair and system maintenance — not pool cleaning. Our focus is on keeping your pool systems running safely and efficiently, from pumps and filters to wiring, electronics, and heaters. We’re a local, owner-operated business serving Brevard County, available 24/7 for diagnostics, repairs, and equipment troubleshooting.

Whether it’s a failing pump, electrical malfunction, or plumbing issue, we bring expert knowledge and honest service to every job. Considering a salt conversion? We’re happy to answer all your questions. If your pool system needs attention, we’re here to get it back in working order. Fast, friendly, and professional service — just a call away.

Services offered:

  • Pump / motor repair & replacement
  • Lighting diagnostics & upgrades
  • Filtration system troubleshooting
  • Salt system conversion & support
  • Heater replacement / install
  • Pool automation setup & service
